Persona and storyboarding

Introduction

I choose the kiosk at MacDonald as the accessible interface for this project. MacDonald is somewhere people go to for a quick meal or when they want something late in the night. I observed and interviewed people using the kiosk to order food and check out. After interviewed three people, I come out with the persona and the storyboard.

A McDonald's ordering kiosk is a self-service terminal that allows customers to place their orders without interacting with a human cashier. The kiosk typically features a touch screen display that customers can use to browse the menu, select items, and customize their orders. Customers can then pay for their orders using cash, credit cards, or mobile payment services, and the kiosk will print out a receipt with the order details. The kiosk may also offer additional features, such as the ability to redeem coupons or place orders for pickup at a later time. Overall, McDonald's ordering kiosks are designed to provide a convenient and efficient way for customers to place their orders.

Recording Observations

Key observations:

  • Users go to the kiosk and start ordering the food without much difficulty.
  • They all start with the start order option on the main screen to let the system begin a new order, and in the drop down menu they guided to the wanted place without much hesitation.
  • They all know what they want and use the system with proficiency.
  • After hover on the menu for a few minutes the costumer begin to check out. Two made the payment by using credit card and user used apply pay. Both payment method are aceepted by the kiosk.
